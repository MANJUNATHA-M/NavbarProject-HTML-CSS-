OBITO UCHIHA

Obito Uchiha, also known by his alias Tobi, is a character in Masashi Kishimoto's manga Naruto. He is first introduced in a "Kakashi Chronicle" side story as a young ninja who sacrifices himself to save his friends after he was crushed by a landslide.
Jutsu: Six Red Yang Formation, Summoning Technique, MORE
Created by: Masashi Kishimoto
First appearance: Naruto manga, chapter 239: "Chronicle 1: The Mission Begins...!!"
Ninja Rank: Chunin
