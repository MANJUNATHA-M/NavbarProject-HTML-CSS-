HINATA HYUGA

Hinata Hyuga is a fictional character in the anime and manga Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. Hinata is a kunoichi and the former heiress of the Hyūga clan from the fictional village of Konohagakure.
Children: Kawaki, Himawari Uzumaki, Boruto Uzumaki
Spouse: Naruto Uzumaki
Created by: Masashi Kishimoto First appearance: Naruto chapter 34: Intruders? (2000)
Ninja rank: Genin in Part I; Chunin in Part II
Ninja team: Team 8/Kurenai Yūhi
