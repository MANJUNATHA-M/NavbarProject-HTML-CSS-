ITACHI UCHIHA

Itachi Uchiha is a character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. Itachi is the older brother of Sasuke Uchiha, and is responsible for killing all the elder members of their clan, sparing only Sasuke. Wikipedia
Clan: Uchiha Clan Notable alias: Ichiha Itachi
Played by: Hideo Ishikawa Akatsuki partner: Kisame Hoshigaki (Rogue ninja)
Created by: Masashi Kishimoto
First appearance: Naruto chapter #139; Naruto episode #81: Return of the morning mist
Relatives: Sasuke Uchiha (younger brother); Sakura Haruno (sister-in-law); Sarada Uchiha (niece).
